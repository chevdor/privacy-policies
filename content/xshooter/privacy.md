This privacy policy explains how XShooter handles your data. By using XShooter, you agree to the practices described below.

## Data Storage

All shooting performance data (shot coordinates, scores, session statistics) is stored locally on your device or on your club's local server. **No data is transmitted to or stored on cloud servers operated by ChevTech.**

## Authentication

XShooter uses OAuth (Google Sign-In) solely to verify your email address. We do not store your password or access other account information.

## Third-Party Services

The app uses the following third-party services:

- **Google Play Services** - for OAuth authentication and app distribution
- **Google Sign-In** - for email verification

These services may collect information according to their own privacy policies:
- [Google Privacy Policy](https://policies.google.com/privacy)

## Log Data

In case of app errors, diagnostic data may be collected including device type, operating system version, app configuration, and timestamps. This data is used solely for troubleshooting.

## Your Data Rights

Since your data is stored locally on your device or your club's server:
- **Access/Export**: Your data is already in your possession
- **Deletion**: Uninstall the app or contact your club administrator
- **Portability**: Contact your club for data stored on their server

## Children's Privacy

XShooter is not intended for users under 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 13.

## Security

We employ industry-standard security practices. However, no method of electronic storage is 100% secure.

## Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy periodically. Changes will be posted on this page.

**Effective date**: 2024-11-24

## About

XShooter is developed by ChevTech and provided "as is" for shooting performance tracking.
